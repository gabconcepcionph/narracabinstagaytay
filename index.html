Your Private Forest Retreat

Nestled just 600 meters away from Tagaytay, Narra Cabins is an exclusive Airbnb set in a beautiful lush garden. Perfect for up to 10 guests, it's like you're surrounded by your own little forest, with hidden corners for when you need some alone time.

This newly-opened cabin features an A-frame with loft-style interiors. Unlike typical A-frame cabins, Narra has bigger spaces where you'll find a living area overlooking the garden, a fully equipped kitchen, and a dining area that can seat 10 people.
